Why Did We Start GreenSorting?

At GreenSorting, we recognized a pressing need in our communities. Conversations with friends and family revealed a common frustration: the overwhelming amount of recyclable materials ending up in landfills. We started GreenSorting to bridge this gap, empowering individuals to recycle effectively while earning rewards.

Our Problems to Solve

Every year, millions of tons of recyclable materials are discarded, contributing to pollution and resource depletion. Our goal at GreenSorting is to tackle this issue head-on. We aim to provide an easy platform for individuals to recycle their materials responsibly while fostering community engagement and awareness about the importance of recycling.
