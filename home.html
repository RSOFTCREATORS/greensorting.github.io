About Us

GreenSorting is a recycling exchange marketplace, an online platform where users trade recyclable items to promote the ideology of “Reusing and Reducing waste.”

Our project addresses a significant issue: nearly 75% of thrown-away items are recyclable. This gap contributes to waste in landfills while preventing communities from helping each other. Instead of discarding items, users can sort recyclable materials to earn money for school expenses.

Our unique selling proposition focuses on empowering users to recycle while earning funds through collaboration with local organizations.

Materials We Recycle

We recycle a variety of materials including:

  • Plastic
  • Paper
  • Cardboard
  • Glass
  • Metals
  • Furniture

By reusing and recycling these materials, we help to reduce waste and promote responsible consumption.
