Our Vision

At GreenSorting, our vision is to create a sustainable future through innovative recycling solutions that empower communities and protect the environment. We strive to lead the way in recycling practices, making it accessible and rewarding for everyone.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide effective recycling solutions that not only reduce waste but also educate and engage individuals about the importance of recycling in protecting our planet. We aim to foster a culture of sustainability by making recycling easy and rewarding for all.

Organization Overview

GreenSorting is a project organization created by three middle schoolers from Indonesia who formed a group called LRC (Learning and Recycling Community). The organization was established with the aim of addressing the pressing environmental issues caused by improper waste disposal and a lack of awareness about recycling. Through innovative programs and community engagement, LRC inspires individuals to adopt sustainable practices in their daily lives.

Our founders, driven by a passion for environmental conservation, have dedicated their efforts to educating peers and community members about the benefits of recycling. They believe that every small action contributes to a larger impact, and through GreenSorting, they seek to create a ripple effect that encourages responsible waste management. By collaborating with local communities, schools, and organizations, GreenSorting aims to build a culture of sustainability that empowers individuals to take action for a cleaner and greener future.
